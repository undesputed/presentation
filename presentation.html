What We Are Building
Not a new project management tool — an AI that connects the tools you already have 新しいツールではなく、既存のツールを繋ぐAI
💬

You talk to it in plain language

No buttons, no dashboards. You type a question or a request — and the AI understands what you need and which tool to check.

Example: "What is blocking the sprint and who should I talk to?"

🔗

It works with tools you already use

The AI connects to Backlog, Slack, and Google Calendar. It reads and writes to these tools for you. Your team does not need to change anything.

Your team keeps their tools — only the PM's experience changes.

🤖

The AI decides what to do

When you ask a question, the AI chooses which tool to check and how to answer. You do not need to know where the information is — just ask.

Example: "What did the team finish this week?" → AI checks Backlog and replies.

⚡

It can take action, not just answer

The AI can also do things for you — create a task, send a Slack message, or book a meeting — all from one instruction.

Example: "Schedule a sprint review and notify the team on Slack."

How the AI Connects Your Tools

📋 Backlog MCP
📅 Google Calendar MCP
💬 Slack MCP

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a standard way for AI to safely read and write to external tools. Each tool has its own MCP server. The AI uses these to get information and take action — without you switching tabs or logging in to anything.

What is MCP?
A simple idea that makes everything connect — no tech knowledge needed すべてを繋ぐシンプルな仕組み
🏝️

The problem: tools that can't talk to each other

Backlog, Slack, and Google Calendar each hold useful information — but they live in separate places. On its own, the AI has no way to get inside any of them. It's like a brilliant assistant who can't open your filing cabinets.

🚪

MCP: a safe door between the AI and each tool

MCP gives each tool a small, secure door that the AI is allowed to use. When you ask a question, the AI quietly knocks on the right door, gets what it needs, and brings the answer back to you — safely and with your permission.

✅

You just ask — the AI handles the rest

You never see or touch MCP. It works quietly in the background, like a bridge you never have to cross yourself. All you do is type a question in plain language, and the AI figures out which door to open.

Think of it this way: MCP is like a universal key ring the AI carries. Each tool has its own lock. The AI picks the right key automatically — you never touch a single lock yourself.
