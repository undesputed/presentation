Frequently asked questions

Which integrations are included in each plan? +

Starter includes up to 5 integrations of your choice. Pro and Enterprise unlock all 19+ integrations — Slack, GitHub, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, ClickUp, monday.com, Asana, Confluence, Backlog, Miro, draw.io, and more.

Can I change or cancel my plan at any time? +

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your workspace settings. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle — no penalties, no lock-in.

Is there a free trial for the Pro plan? +

Yes — every new workspace starts with a 14-day Pro trial, no credit card required. At the end of the trial you can subscribe to continue with Pro, or you'll automatically move to the Starter free tier.

How does workspace billing work? +

Pricing is per workspace per month. A single account can have multiple workspaces — for example, separate ones for different clients, teams, or projects — and each workspace is billed independently at the plan rate.

How is my data and OAuth token stored? +

All OAuth tokens for connected integrations are stored encrypted at rest using AES-256 (PostgreSQL pgp_sym_encrypt). Your conversation data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level isolation per workspace. Enterprise plans support on-premise deployment for full data residency control.
